1. SLAVE DRIVER
You make everyone toe the mark—right down to the last centimeter. You exact blind, unquestioning obedience; demand the strictest conformity to rules, however arbitrary or tyrannical; and will not tolerate the slightest deviation from your orders. You are, in short, the very epitome of the army drill sergeant.
You are a

2. BOOTLICKER
You toady to rich or influential people, catering to their vanity, flattering their ego. You are the personification of the traditional ward heeler, you out-yes the Hollywood yes men. And on top of all these unpleasant characteristics, you're a complete hypocrite. All your servile attentions spring from selfish desires to get ahead.
You are a

Your artistic efforts are simply a means of passing time pleasantly.

You're strong-minded, unyielding, sharp-tongued, and dangerous. You can curse like a stevedore and yell like a fishwife—and often do.

5. SUPERPATRIOT
Anything you own or belong to is better—simply because you own it or belong to it. Your religion is far superior to any other; your political party is the only honest one; your neighborhood puts all others in the city in the shade. In short, you are exaggeratedly, aggressively, absurdly devoted to your own affiliations.
You are a
chauvinist

martinet
mahr-tə-NET'
A strict disciplinarian; one who demands rigid adherence to rules and unquestioning obedience. An inflexible authority figure.
Related Forms: martinetish (adj.), martinetism (n.)
sycophant
SIK'-ə-fənt
A toady; a flatterer; one who acts obsequiously to people of wealth or power in hopes of personal gain. A hypocrite who conceals selfish motives behind false adulation.
Related Forms: sycophancy (n.), sycophantic (adj.)
dilettante
dil'-ə-TANT
A dabbler in the arts; one who pursues an artistic or intellectual interest superficially for amusement rather than serious study or professional competence. An amateur without depth of commitment.
Related Forms: dilettantish (adj.), dilettantism (n.); Plural: dilettantes or dilettanti
virago
və-RAH'-go
A loud-mouthed, shrewish, aggressive woman; one who is quarrelsome, domineering, and entirely lacking in gentleness. A woman of masculine vigor and harsh demeanor.
Related Forms: viragos (plural), viraginal (adj.)
chauvinist
SHŌ'-və-nist
A blatant superpatriot; one excessively and aggressively devoted to his own country, group, or affiliations. One who believes his own beliefs, country, religion, or party is superior to all others.
Related Forms: chauvinism (n.), chauvinistic (adj.)
monomaniac
mon'-ə-MAY'-nee-ak
A person with a single obsessive fixation or mania; one whose entire focus is consumed by a single interest or pursuit. A fanatic with tunnel vision of purpose.
Related Forms: monomania (n.), monomaniacal (adj.)
iconoclast
i-KON'-ə-klast'
One who attacks cherished traditions, revered beliefs, and established customs. A breaker of idols; a reformer who challenges conventional wisdom and sacred institutions.
Related Forms: iconoclasm (n.), iconoclastic (adj.)
atheist
AY'-thee-ist
One who denies the existence of God; a disbeliever in the divine or supernatural. One whose worldview is based entirely on non-religious, secular reasoning.
Related Forms: atheism (n.), atheistic (adj.), atheistically (adv.)
lecher
LECH'-ər
A sexually predatory male; one who is lascivious and lustful, making unwanted sexual advances without moral restraint. A libertine consumed by base desires.
Related Forms: lechery (n.), lecherous (adj.), lecherously (adv.)
hypochondriac
hī'-pə-KON'-dree-ak
One who suffers from imaginary or exaggerated ailments; a person convinced of serious illness despite medical evidence to the contrary. A chronic worrier about one's health.
Related Forms: hypochondria (n.), hypochondriasis (n.), hypochondriacal (adj.)
